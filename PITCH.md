# LaunchLens: Validate Startup Ideas 5X Faster with AI

## 🎯 The Problem

**90% of startups fail. Most fail because founders built the wrong thing.**

### Why?
1. **Validation takes too long**
   - Traditional approach: 6+ weeks of manual research
   - Talk to customers? Schedule calls, take notes, synthesize findings
   - Research competitors? Google, visit sites, compile spreadsheets
   - Analyze market size? Industry reports, extrapolation, guessing
   - Result: Months wasted before finding out there's no real demand

2. **Founders make it up**
   - No structured validation process
   - Lean Canvas is good but requires self-discipline
   - Most founders skip it or do it wrong
   - Confirmation bias: find data that supports their idea

3. **Market research is expensive**
   - Industry reports: $1000+
   - Customer research: requires budget
   - Competitor analysis: time-consuming
   - Cost of doing it wrong: months of salary burn

4. **Timing is critical**
   - By the time you validate, market may have moved
   - Competitors may have already filled the gap
   - You've burned capital and energy

## ✨ The Solution: LaunchLens

**Validate your startup idea in 15 minutes with real data + AI analysis**

### What LaunchLens Does

A 5-step AI-powered framework that validates startup ideas fast:

```
1. DECOMPOSE IDEA
   Input: "Local juice bar in Plano, Texas"
   ↓ AI breaks down into:
   └─ Business type, location, target customers, pricing, channels to research

2. DISCOVER INSIGHTS
   ↓ Real market research using Google + AI:
   ├─ What do people want? (real Reddit posts + Google search)
   ├─ What pain points exist? (direct customer language)
   ├─ Who are competitors? (pricing, positioning, gaps)
   └─ Verdict: 47 signals of high demand (score: 7.8/10)

3. ANALYZE MARKET
   ↓ Detailed analysis of 5 dimensions:
   ├─ Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM): $1.2B plausible market
   ├─ Competitors: 23 juice bars, 3 direct competitors, clear gaps
   ├─ Customer Segments: 3 viable personas with pain points
   ├─ Root Causes: Why existing solutions fail
   └─ Launch Costs: $50K-120K to get started

4. SETUP PLAN
   ↓ Practical launch roadmap:
   ├─ Equipment suppliers: Marblehead, Omega, Champion (with links)
   ├─ Team: Need manager, juice technician, delivery driver
   ├─ Timeline: 8-12 weeks to launch
   └─ Cost breakdown: Rent, equipment, licenses, initial inventory

5. VALIDATE STRATEGY
   ↓ Test-before-you-build plan:
   ├─ Landing page template (designed for conversions)
   ├─ Survey questions (validated for juice bars)
   ├─ 5 Reddit communities to target
   ├─ WhatsApp outreach script
   ├─ Success metrics: 150 waitlist signups = strong signal

6. TRACK EXPERIMENTS
   ↓ Real results captured:
   ├─ Run landing page test: 87 signups
   ├─ Send survey: 23 responses (74% would switch)
   ├─ Community outreach: 450 upvotes
   └─ Verdict: GO ✓ (Strong demand, proceed with confidence)
```

## 🛠️ How It Works (Technical)

### Data Sources
1. **Real Market Research**
   - Google Search (via Serper.dev): competitor data, trends, market size
   - Reddit API: actual customer pain points and desires
   - Real-time pricing lookups

2. **AI-Powered Analysis**
   - Groq LLaMA 3.3 (fastest LLM): primary analysis
   - Google Gemini Flash: fallback if Groq rate-limited
   - Custom prompts for each stage

3. **Structured Outputs**
   - JSON responses (not natural language)
   - Validated scores and thresholds
   - Linked data (suppliers, communities, competitors)

### Two-Mode UX
- **Explore Mode** (no signup): Run full validation, all data local
- **Save Mode** (with signup): Store experiments, track history, compare verdicts

### Verdict Engine
```
GO ✓        → signups ≥150 AND switch_rate ≥60% AND price ≥$8
PIVOT ⚠      → Some strong signals but constraints exist
KILL 🛑       → signups <30 AND switch_rate <30%
AWAITING ?   → No results entered yet
```
